Mobile App for sustainable clothes sharing.

Summary

Project Brief

This individual assignment, issued by Thomas More, challenged us to create a digital solution that promotes sustainability within the fashion industry. The project required in-depth research on sustainable fashion practices, the development of a concept based on our findings, and the design and implementation of a functional application. We were asked to build at least five screens, visually present the concept through pitch materials and pamphlets, and convincingly communicate its value through a short verbal pitch.

Solution

Toda is a peer-to-peer clothing exchange app designed to make sustainable fashion easy and social. Instead of buying new outfits for every special occasion, users can share pieces from their existing wardrobe with friends, keeping their style fresh without contributing to overconsumption. The platform encourages a more mindful approach to fashion by reducing waste, extending the life cycle of clothing, and making it easier to access variety without constant purchasing. Through intuitive design and a community-driven model, Toda supports a shift toward more responsible and conscious fashion habits.

Branding

Primary Logo

The logo captures Toda’s spirit: friendly, expressive, sustainable, and centered on connection.

The creative use the "a" adds personality and warmth to the brand, that way it reinforces Toda's focus on joyful sharing. The vibrant mustard yellow symbolises optimism, energy, and a grounded sense of sustainability. In other words: fun yet conscious. It avoids the typical green clichés of eco-design while still nodding to environmental values. The simplicity and memorability of the logo make it instantly recognisable, an important trait for building trust in a new app.

Alternative Logo

A key feature is the “a” in the logo, which doubles as an alternative logo and a charming mascot. This character adds personality to the brand and is ideally suited for smaller contexts, such as icons, tooltips, or in-app guidance.

Typography

Toda uses fonts that reflect the app’s playful yet modern personality, while simultaneously ensuring readability and visual hierarchy across all content.

  • Header One – Modak, 48pt

    • Modak is bold, rounded, and full of character and therefor aligned with TODA’s friendly and expressive brand identity. Its organic shapes and fun aesthetic make it ideal for large titles, key moments and capture attention.




  • Poppins – Black 32pt (H2), Medium 20pt (H3), Regular 14pt (Body)

    • Poppins is a clean, geometric sans-serif that balances Modak’s playfulness with clarity and modernity.
